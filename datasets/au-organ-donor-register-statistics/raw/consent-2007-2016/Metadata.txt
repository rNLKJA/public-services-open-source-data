*  All Consent Registration (Including 16-17yr Intent) data since 2007  
  
*  Tables are divided into Total, Male and Female (Male & Female have specific age ranges)
  

*  Legally Valid Consent Registrations (Including Intent Registrations of 16 & 17 year olds) = Female Total + Male Total.  
  
* % of ABS Estimated Population (as at 30 June 2014) = Gender Total/ABS Estimated Gender Population.  **Please note:**  Excludes estimated population of 0-15 year old residents.  
  
*  State % of Legally Valid Consent Registrations (Including Intent Registrations of 16 & 17 year olds) = Total Legally Valid Consent Registrations (Including Intent Registrations of 16 & 17 year olds) for State / Total Legally Valid Consent Registrations  
  
*  Negative Variance occurs due to: registration end-dated due to death or by request, registrant moved to another state.  
  
*  Tables include registrants who have registered their objection to donate - 18 years & above. Specific figures for each month available in the associated XLSX files published on Data.gov.au
