Australian Organ Donor Register statistics

Data about registrations on the Australian Organ Donor Register.

on this page

  • Total Legally Valid Consent Registrations (Including Intent Registrations of 16 and 17 year olds) as at 31 May 2026
  • Intent Registrations as at 31 May 2026

The Australian Organ Donor Register (the Donor Register) is the national register for people to record their decision about becoming an organ and tissue donor for transplantation after death.

The Donor Register allows authorised medical personnel to verify a person’s decision about donating their organs and tissue for transplantation.

Registrations are arranged by age group, state and gender.

Total registrations as at 31 May 2026: 7,997,563.
